Honkai: Star Rail

Overview of Honkai: Star Rail

Honkai: Star Rail is Mihoyo's newest released title where players can explore a fictional cosmos aboard the Astral Express.

  • See the sights on multiple fantastical locales from the frozen wasteland of Jarilo VI to the wandering planet-sized craft of the Luofu.
  • Play with a variety of characters, from the frozen Sunshine of March 7th to the cutting edge of Blade!
  • Experience the cultures amongst the stars, planets, and satellites of an entire galaxy!
  • Engage with riveting turn based combat where every decision counts towards victory.
  • See new content on a regular basis as the game is actively expanded on, including a consistent release of events!
